Project UI/UX/Case Study

Surface

We are going to review the UI/UX project I completed for my user experience class. For this project, I was assigned to develop a mobile app designed to sell a product and include an augmented reality feature. I created a brand called 'Surface,' which specializes in selling and curating graphic design posters. What sets Surface apart is its focus on curating work from underground graphic designers and producing high-quality printed graphic posters. With Surface, I integrated the augmented reality feature to allow users to visualize how their chosen poster would look with a selected frame in their real environment.
