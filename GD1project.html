Project / Case Study:

Pacific Calm

I'm a big fan of turning ideas into reality, so I'm really into the concept of brand identity. I enjoy developing an entire look and feel for a brand and seeing how everything comes together. For my Graphic Design Project, I chose one of Lindsy Marsh's Logo Challenges from her Graphic Design courses, and instead of just designing a logo, I created a complete brand identity for the fictional company, Pacific Calm.
