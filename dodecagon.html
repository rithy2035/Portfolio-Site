Dodecagon

"Dodecagon" is a word puzzle game app. It was developed for a team production class where I collaborated with two other students to create the app. The version I'm showcasing here is my own creation. We integrated elements from different sources to produce the final version, but I wanted to highlight my version specifically. In my approach, I experimented with 3D tools in Illustrator to develop a modern, visually appealing version of the game. Check it out below!
