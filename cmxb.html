Byredo

I have a strong passion for creative culture, including fashion, art, music, and film. I enjoy incorporating these interests into my work whenever possible. For my very first app design class, I chose to create an app for a fashion article that caught my eye. Photographer Craig McDean collaborated with luxury brand Byredo to launch an apparel collection in celebration of the release of McDean's photography book. I thought it would be interesting to develop a unique app that showcases and sells this collaborative collection. You can find the details of the process below.
